Our Mission

We are HOUToad, a nonprofit organization located at the heart of Houston Texas, willing to protect the Houston Toads from going extinct! Protecting and growing their population is our top priority. For the past 3 years we have been breeding our local Houston Toads to repopulate their species. Help us save our toads by learning about them or by contributing a donation to our company so we could continue seeing our backyard friends for generations to come.
