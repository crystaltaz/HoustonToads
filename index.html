HOU TOAD

Welcome, we are the HOUToad Foundation willing to protect the Houston Toads from going extinct. Protecting and growing their population is our top priority. Help us save our toads by learning about them or by contributing a donation so we could continue seeing our backyard friends for generations to come.

Upcoming Events

Join us to release Houston Toads into the wild this coming fall. Save the date for Saturday, October 14th, at 7:30 pm. We will be at Hermann Park by the bridge. We'll see you there!
